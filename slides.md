# TrackWrapped Slide Sequence

This document outlines the current sequence of slides in the TrackWrapped story experience.

## 1. Welcome Slide (`WelcomeSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Introduces the athlete to their 2025 Wrapped experience.
*   **Content**: "TRACK WRAPPED 2025", Athlete Name, Nickname (if available).
*   **Visual**: Bold typography, neon accents, energetic entrance.

## 2. Veteran Status Slide (`VeteranStatusSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Acknowledges the athlete's experience level.
*   **Content**: Years active (e.g., "ROOKIE" or "VETERAN"), "X YEARS IN THE GAME".
*   **Visual**: Badge-style display, dynamic text scaling.

## 3. Discipline Slide (`DisciplineSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Highlights the athlete's primary event focus.
*   **Content**: Main Discipline (e.g., "400m Hurdles"), Category Icon (Sprint, Distance, etc.).
*   **Visual**: Large icon, bold discipline name, category-specific styling.

## 4. Performance Slide (`PerformanceSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Summarizes the season's overall performance score.
*   **Content**: "PERFORMANCE SCORE" (Average WA Points), Global Ranking, Comparison to Last Season (e.g., "+15%").
*   **Visual**: Massive score number, ranking badge, up/down trend indicator.

## 5. Progression Slide (`ProgressionSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Visualizes the season's performance journey ("THE CLIMB").
*   **Content**: Line graph of all race results, Season Best (SB) highlighted, "X RACES THIS SEASON".
*   **Visual**: Neon line graph, "Race-in" animation, dates on X-axis, Best result at top.

## 6. Ranking Slide (`RankingSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Celebrates the athlete's standing on the global stage ("WORLD STAGE").
*   **Content**: Best World Ranking (e.g., "#42"), Discipline, Points, "TOP X% GLOBAL".
*   **Visual**: Laurel wreaths, gold/neon accents, heroic typography.

## 7. Competition Slide (`CompetitionSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Displays competition frequency and intensity.
*   **Content**: "RACE MODE", Total Competitions, Heatmap of race frequency.
*   **Visual**: Calendar/Grid heatmap style, "ON FIRE" status if frequent.

## 8. Travel Slide (`TravelSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Showcases the athlete's global footprint ("ON TOUR").
*   **Content**: Number of countries competed in, list of flags/countries.
*   **Visual**: World map or list style, "ROCKSTAR" vibe for many countries.

## 9. World Record Slide (`WorldRecordSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Puts the athlete's best performance in perspective with the WR.
*   **Content**: Athlete's PB vs. World Record, "X% OF WR".
*   **Visual**: Comparison bar or circle, "CHASING GREATNESS" theme.

## 10. Nemesis Slide (`NemesisSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Introduces a playful rivalry.
*   **Content**: "YOUR NEMESIS", Opponent Name, Head-to-Head record (e.g., "2-1").
*   **Visual**: "VS" battle card style, dramatic reveal.

## 11. Rivals Slide (`RivalsSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: Lists top competitors faced during the season.
*   **Content**: Top 3 Rivals, "RIVALRY DEFINES US".
*   **Visual**: List format, aggressive/competitive styling.

## 12. Share Card Slide (`ShareCardSlide.vue`)
*   **Purpose**: The final summary card designed for social sharing.
*   **Content**: "2025 SEASON", Season Best, Top Events, Total Races, Heatmap, "TRACK WRAPPED".
*   **Visual**: Highly stylized, dense with info, "Share" button prompt.
